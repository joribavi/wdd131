Website Plan WDD131- Jonathan Villegas

Site Name

The name of the website will be https://joribavi.github.io/wdd131/cusco-travel-agency.html It will be focused on tourist services offered in the City of Cusco, Peru and it will give a basic summary of the company history and goals.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website will be to get the attention of travelers that are interested in visiting Cusco and its surroundings with the assistance of professionals

Scenarios

  • What are the best activities in Cusco?
  • How can I find a reliable travel agency in Peru?
